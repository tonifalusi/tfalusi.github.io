Welcome to Emmaus

Emmaus Christian School is a Pre-school to Year 10 School and a member of CEN (Christian Education National www.cen.edu.au

Located in the established leafy, northern suburb of Dickson, it provides a high quality education and committed to providing a balanced program of spiritual, emotiona, physical and academic nurturing to all it's students.

We have passionate teachers, caring adminstrative staff, enthusiastic students and a fantasitic parent communiyt who all work together to make this a wonderful place. If you would like to find out more, or are interested in enrolling your child, please contact our Enrollments Officer who can answer questions and make a time for you to come and visit our School.
